About Six Majesty

Welcome to Six Majesty, where we specialize in unique, one-of-a-kind
furniture pieces designed to make your home stand out.

Treasure Your Home with Timeless Finds

Our collection features exquisite second-hand Japanese furniture.

Embrace the beauty and craftsmanship of Japan while giving these treasures a new home!

How we Started

Six Majesty was started in the year 2020, where the De Dios Family
got together to start a new business in the wake of the pandemic.
Starting off with small items, stored in their own homes.
They got enough money to buy their own storage space, which they
then took the opportunity to buy larger items like sofas and cabinets.

As the business became larger, they now bought a second storage place
to store more items. But with more items being posted every week, items become
buried under every new posts. This sites hopes to help buyers be able to browse
for furniture that are still available.
